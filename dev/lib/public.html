The device struct must at least have the fields deviceID, params and dependencies and all other fields should have default values.

MPIMeasurements.Sequence ‚Äî Type

Description of a sequence that can be run by a scanner.

The sequence can either be continuous or triggered. Triggered in this context means that the acquisition is done on a certain event, e.g. the move of a robot. The sweeping of frequencies or movement points can also be done in a triggered or continuous fashion.

MPIMeasurements.setActiveChannel ‚Äî Method

Sets the active channels. The parameter channel encodes the switched on and off channels. The bits of the ASCII encoded hexadecimal bytes are the channels, with bit 0 representing channel 1 to bit 7 for channel 8.

For example channel="1E" will switch on channels 2, 3, 4, and 5 and switch off the remaining channels 1,6,7, and 8.
